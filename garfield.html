The Garfield Movie

Exclusively in Movie Theaters This Summer

About

Genres: Family, Animation

Garfield (voiced by Chris Pratt), the world-famous, Monday-hating, lasagna-loving indoor cat, is about to have an out-of-this-world adventure! After an unexpected reunion with his long-lost father, Vic (voiced by Samuel L. Jackson), Garfield and his canine friend Odie are forced from their pretty pampered life into joining Vic in a hilarious, high-stakes heist.

Directed by: Mark Dindal

Screenplay by: Paul A. Kaplan & Mark Torgove and David Reynolds

Cast: Chris Pratt, Samuel L. Jackson, Hannah Waddingham, Ving Rhames, Nicholas Hoult, Cecily Strong, Harvey Guillén, Bret Goldstein, Bowen Yang
