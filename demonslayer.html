About

Following the events of the television series "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba," the movie "Mugen Train" follows Tanjiro Kamado, his sister Nezuko, and their friends Zenitsu and Inosuke as they board the Infinity Train to investigate a series of mysterious disappearances. They join forces with the Flame Hashira, Kyojuro Rengoku, to face powerful demons, including the lower rank demon Enmu.
