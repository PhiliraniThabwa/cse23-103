The Boys

About

Genres:

2019 (ongoing) | Superhero, Action, Black Comedy, Drama

"The Boys" is a satirical take on the superhero genre, set in a world where superheroes, known as "Supes," are recognized as heroes by the general public but are often corrupt and self-serving in reality. The series follows a vigilante group known as "The Boys," who aim to take down corrupt superheroes with no more than their blue-collar grit and a willingness to fight dirty.
